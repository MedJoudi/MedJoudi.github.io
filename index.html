About Me

Ever since I was in high school, I have been deeply passionate about computers and how they work. This fascination inspired me to pursue a degree in computer science, which I studied for several years.
During my studies, I developed a particular interest in multimedia and subsequently specialized in this area at the ISI Kef in Tunisia. There, I gained in-depth knowledge and skills in multimedia design, development, and production.
Building upon my previous studies, I pursued further studies at the Eszterhazy Karoly Fiscola in Hungary, where I focused on application programming for two years. This experience allowed me to deepen my understanding of programming concepts and their practical applications in the development of software systems.
I was honored to be awarded the national certification of superior technician in the field of multimedia in Tunis, in recognition of my achievements.
Currently, I am eager to expand my knowledge and skills in the exciting field of cloud computing, as I believe it offers great potential for innovation and growth.
